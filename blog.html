How to make & bake your own Cinnamon Rolls!

What you will need?

Dry Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 or 630g Gluten-Free Flour
  • 1 tsp or 6g Salt
  • 1 package or 7g Instant Yeast
  • 1/2 Cup or 100g Sugar

Wet Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups or 473ml Regular or Dairy-Free Milk, warmed to 105 degrees
  • 2/3 Cups Butter or DF Margarine, melted

Filling

  • 1 Cup or 200g Brown Sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/3 Cup Melted Butter or Margarine

Step-by-step: How to make your cinnamon rolls?

  1. Mix together dry ingredients in stand mixer for 30 seconds.
  2. Slowly add in wet ingredients.
  3. Mix on med-high in stand mixer for 6-8 minutes.
  4. Cover bowl with a plastic wrap or damp towel, then rest for 30-40 minutes.
  5. While dough is rising, prepare the filling for later use.
  6. Using a rolling mat or 2 pieces of parchment paper, make a 16 x 24 surface.
  7. Flour surface, then turn dough out onto the floured surface.
  8. Gently roll while sprinkling flour as needed, until dough reaches 16x24.
  9. Spread filling onto the dough.
  10. Starting at the long side, roll to make into a long log.
  11. Cut the log into 12 equal rolls. Place into baking dish lined with parchment paper.
  12. Sprinkle with water, and allow to rise for 45-60 seconds (Double in size).
  13. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes
  14. Enjoy!
